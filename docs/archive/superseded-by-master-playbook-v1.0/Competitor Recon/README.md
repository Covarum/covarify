# Competitor Recon

This folder contains dated research on adjacent products and market patterns. The goal is not to copy competitors, but to understand what users already expect, which claims are crowded, and where Covarify must create a meaningfully different outcome.

## Current review

- [July 16, 2026 - Covarify Market Position Review](./2026-07-16-market-position.md)

## Research rules

- Prefer current official product, pricing, help-center, and policy sources.
- Record the review date and refresh trigger.
- Separate observed product facts from strategic inference.
- Do not call a capability unique without checking the current market.
- Move durable strategic conclusions into the living playbook and decision log.
